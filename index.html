Explore the Mountain Dew Flavors over the years of production!

Flavors

Click on the one of your choice to learn more about it!

Click here to vote on the best DEW (New Tab)

Regular

True to the green brand and what started it all. This product has be in production for over 80 plus years.

Voltage

This is a raspberry citurus flavor that is colored dark blue.

Pitch Black

This flavor has been off and on since 2004 and is a grape-ish flavor delight.

Baja Blast

This flavor is a Taco Bell exclusive but over the last couple of years is now available on store shelves althought with the Taco Bell logo. For taste, it can be described as a "tropical lime storm." This is in my opionion the best flavor.

Code Red

The cherry flavor alternative to the green brand.

Live Wire

More citrus but in orange. Can't go wrong.

Throwback

— Cane Sugar—

I find this flavor the best of all. No high frutose cornsyrup is added but instead is made with actual cane sugar. The flavor has a more sweeter taste in my opionion. This are found in both plastic and glass bottles.

Label Series

Black, White, and Green - or Dark Berry, Tropical Citrus, and Green Apple Kiwi respectively

Spiked Series

The name may suggest it is alcholic but it is not (unfortunately). These are two lemonade flavors - regular and raspberry.

Kickstart Series

Low Carbonated, low sugar (compared to Mt Dew atleast) juice for the most part that can provide the needed jump in the morning.

Enjoy!
